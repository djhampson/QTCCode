5. What are the key business issues and drivers?

Question
What is the point of Pain the customer needs to address? What are the internal and external drivers impacting their ability to succeed?
Objective
To determine what is affecting the ACCOUNT from both an internal and external point of view.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. To be able to explain the major business drivers for the ACCOUNT and what affect these are having.
  2. To have validated this ‘scenario’ with key influencers and decision makers in the ACCOUNT.
Example
  • No view of financial performance across the enterprise.
  • Disparate and out-dated systems that make it hard to transact with and within (inter-company is crucial for them)
  • Need systems to support strategic aims (e.g. enabling customer self-service)
  • Move to Cloud
