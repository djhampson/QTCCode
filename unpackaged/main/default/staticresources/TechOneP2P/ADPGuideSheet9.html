9. Business Relationship

Question
Do YOU have a business relationship goal? (Supplier, preferred supplier, trusted advisor or business partner). Which is YOUR relationship strategy? With which of the key decision makers are YOU appropriately positioned? Who are YOUR immediate targets in the political map?
Objective
To determine an appropriate relationship objective and to ensure YOU have a network of key contacts in the ACCOUNT through which you can demonstrate the value YOU offer and who can drive the decision making processes of the major opportunities that YOU will be pursuing.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. That you have determined YOUR current supplier status.
  2. That you have determined YOUR objective for supplier status.
  3. That YOU have mapped the formal organization charts.
  4. That YOU have mapped the matrix of relationships between YOU and YOUR contacts within the ACCOUNT.
  5. That YOU have mapped the informal relationships that exist.
  6. That YOU have developed a relationship development plan that leverages the informal relationships in the ACCOUNT.
