The Account Overview

Purpose
To give a snap shot of progress of the Plan2Prosper at any given time and to act as a briefing document to the virtual team, business management and the Executive.
Description
The ACCOUNT Overview has four sections
  1. The Introduction: - A short ‘heads up’ of who is in the virtual account team; what the current sales opportunities are; the year to date performance and forecast and the ‘headroom’ available in the account.
  2. The Account Summary which focuses on the current sales performance; an overview of past value delivered and significant wins and losses and a commentary on progress this year to-date.
  3. The Customer Profile which details information about the ACCOUNT; about their market; their competition; their business drivers (challenges); their strategic intent; and the responses(major initiatives) that they are putting in place to overcome their challenges.
  4. YOUR Goals and Ambitions in terms of the long term sustainable mutual business value for YOU and YOUR client; your relationship development plan, the vital few projects that you need to deliver in order to attain the mutual business value; YOUR objectives in the current fiscal year and finally the short term (90 Day) action plan.
Steps in the Process
The ACCOUNT Overview is built over time from information gleaned from the 1 to 9 and from information that YOU establish elsewhere.
  1. Continue to review and complete the evidence section of each of the 1-9 Questions. The answers given then populate relevant sections of the Account Overview.
  2. Complete the additional sections in the Account Overview section.
Considerations
  • The Account Overview is an amalgam of information retained from evidence in the 3x3 and from YOUR general knowledge regarding both ACCOUNT and their market.
  • The Account Overview is the summary document of YOUR Plan2Prosper and as such is the primary communication document.
