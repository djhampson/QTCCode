6. Win Themes?

Question
Have we identified the hierarchy of win themes?
Objective
To identify win themes which articulate the things the customer values and may not be formally described in their docs.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
Look at things like:
  1. Relationships.
  2. What’s important about the vendor?
  3. Combatting issues with change.
  4. Industry drivers/external pressures.
  5. Reducing cost of maintaining systems.
