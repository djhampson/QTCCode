7. Have we identified the success fundamentals?

Question
What are the critical requirements of the customer. What do they value above all else?
Objective
To determine the hierarchy and if possible Critical to Quality (C2Q) needs of the client for the range of product and services that YOU offer in order for YOU to identify compelling reasons to purchase YOUR products.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. That YOU have ‘triangulated’ the hierarchy of needs within the target ACCOUNT.
  2. That YOU understand the relative importance of this hierarchy of needs.
  3. That this hierarchy of needs is formally recognised by both key influencers and economic decision makers.
Example
  • No change of key team members in account/PM
  • Deliver what we promise
  • Keep close to XXXX as she is the major influencer
