3. What are the opportunities?

Question
What is the customer's perception of Advantages and Opportunities of working with T1?
Objective
To look at our strengths and see if they open up any opportunities?
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  • Establish the following:
    1. What good opportunities can you spot?
    2. What interesting trends are you aware of?
  • Useful opportunities can come from such things as:
    1. Changes in technology and markets on both a broad and narrow scale
    2. Changes in government policy related to your field
    3. Changes in social patterns, population profiles, lifestyle changes, and so on
    4. Local events
    Example
    Introduction of Smart Mobile devices to increase staff efficiency, productivity and satisfaction
