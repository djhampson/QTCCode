8. Relationship Status

Question
Do the key influencers and economic decision makers in the ACCOUNT know about the past value YOU have delivered, the major wins YOU have gained and the positive service experience they have had?
Objective
To ensure that the key influencers and decision makers understand the value YOU can bring and are aware of the resources that are available to YOU.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. Prove that YOU have delivered significant business value to the ACCOUNT.
  2. Detail the significant wins and losses.
  3. Highlight the resolution of major service issues.
  4. Demonstrate that the key influencers and economic buyers are aware of these facts.
