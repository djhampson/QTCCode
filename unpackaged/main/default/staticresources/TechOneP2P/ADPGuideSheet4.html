4. What are the threats?

Question
What is the customer's perception of T1 in regard to Risks and Value Propostion relative to other vendors?
Objective
To look at the threats that exist to our organisation in the market?
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  • Establish the following:
    1. What obstacles do you face?
    2. What are your competitors doing?
    3. Are quality standards or specifications for your job, products or services changing?
    4. Is changing technology threatening your position?
    5. Do you have bad debt or cash-flow problems?
    6. Could any of your weaknesses seriously threaten your business?
    Example
    "Best of Breed approach is viewed as better than an Enterprise approach"
