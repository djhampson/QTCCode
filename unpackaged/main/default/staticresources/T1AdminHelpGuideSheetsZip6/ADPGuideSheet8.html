8. Critical Needs

Question
Can we identify the critical needs of the customer and what is their priority?
Objective
To determine the customer critical needs and their priority for the range of products and services that we offer in order for us to identify compelling reasons to purchase our products/services.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. That we have ‘triangulated’ the hierarchy of needs within the target customer.
  2. That we understand the relative importance of this hierarchy of needs.
  3. That this hierarchy of needs is formally recognised by both key influencers and economic decision makers.
Examples
  • The customer has the following priority of needs with respect to the product and services we supply:
    1. Move to Cloud
    2. Implement a document management system
    3. Integrate reporting
Considerations
  • Consider all the product mix in terms of product functionality/quality, price, service, and support.
