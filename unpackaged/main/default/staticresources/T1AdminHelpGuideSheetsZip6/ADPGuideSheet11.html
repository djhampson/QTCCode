11. The Vital Few

Question
Have you determined the ‘Vital Few’ ambition goals? What are their timeline and milestones?
Objective
To determine the key milestones (other than business opportunities) that will drive our progress in the customer.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. That you have determined our Sustainable Mutual Business Value (SMBV).
  2. That you have a series of clear objectives in (but not limited to):
    • Key sales opportunities
    • Q1: Significant Value Derived
    • Q4: Relationship Status
    • Q12: Fiscal Objectives (as opposed to sales opportunities)
    • Q16: Permission
  3. That you have a plan of action to achieve the milestone objectives.
Examples
  • Key milestones of:
    • To have been recognised by senior executives as having contributed a 30% improvement in sales productivity through the past three years.
    • To have secured a meeting between YOUR CEO and the customers CEO.
    • To have reached sales of $500K.
Considerations
  • Sales opportunities that are regarded as being milestones are those that demonstrate our ability to deliver the SMBV.
  • It is essential that the key milestones demonstrate progress toward the relationship status that YOU have targeted.
  • There can be more than one milestone objective per question.
  • There can be no milestone objective for certain questions.
Links
  • Sales Opportunities in the Account Overview to review those currently in progress and select the ones that are important in terms of progressing you Sustainable Mutual Business Value (SMBV) and to attaining the Account Status objectives.
  • The objectives in the 1 to 16 Questions in order to review those that are key to demonstrate the SMBV and to attaining the Account Status objectives
