4. Business Responses

Question
Have we identified the customers planned business responses to the business drivers over the next three years? With whom and what evidence do you have to claim this?
Objective
To identify where the focus is going to be over the next period (three years) in order that you can understand which areas you can help the customer address.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. You have identified formal business responses by the customer to address key business drivers.
  2. The customer has committed finance and resource to address the business driver.
  3. There is a time line with regard the identified response.
  4. The response has been confirmed by key influencers and decision makers.
Examples
  • A formal transformation programme has been announced, the objective of which is to attain market leadership, growing revenue by X and margin by Y by the year XXXX. The transformation programme has global sponsors with certain key executives taking ownership of certain work streams.
Considerations
  • Ensure YOU ‘triangulate’ the business response with the sponsoring Executive(s). Operational staff are targeted with delivery, but changes in the circumstances of the customer account can result in rapid shifts in focus between tactical priorities and strategic ones.
