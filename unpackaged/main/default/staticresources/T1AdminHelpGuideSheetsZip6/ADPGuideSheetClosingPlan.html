The Plan2Propser

Purpose
To document the account development plan and actions required - the Plan2Prosper.
Description
The Plan2Prosper is a record of the objectives, tasks and activities that have been decided in answering the 16 Questions. It is organized in two different views:
  • A view of objectives with corresponding activities.
  • A summary view of activities.
Steps in the Process
  1. Select the tab by which YOU wish to view the plan; objectives or activities.
  2. If the chosen view is objectives, select the view that you wish to see; open, completed or all objectives.
  3. If all objectives is selected then select ‘show activities’ to highlight activities for the individual questions.
  4. If the chosen view is activities, then simply view the activities by date order.
  5. If the chosen view is the Schedule of Events then sense check the scheduled completion dates by passing each objective through the filters of ‘urgency and importance’. Each objective will then be given a priority and you can re-schedule the objective depending on where the priorities lie.
    You can also refer to the matrix by which you have evaluated the level of importance and urgency and take the tactical steps of Do, Defer, Diminish or Delete.
  6. If the chosen view is The Plan, then refer to the Gantt chart and ensure that all actions can be achieved with in the timeframe of the schedule completion date of the objective, changing the timing of each of the objectives and activities as necessary by dragging and each end of the activity bar.
  7. Once an action is completed select ‘closed’ and then ‘save’ in order to record the fact.
  8. Once all activities are completed, go to the objective and then select ‘closed’ and ‘save’ in order to record the fact.
Considerations
  • You cannot close an objective until all activities relating to that objective are completed.
