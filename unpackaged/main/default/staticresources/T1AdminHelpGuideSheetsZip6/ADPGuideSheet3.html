3. Service History

Question
Are there no, or are we successfully managing any issues (past and present) that have affected or may affect our relationship(s) within the customer?
Objective
To ensure there is no outstanding product, service or administration issues that the customer feels are not being addressed and which may impact our credibility.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. That YOU have established with key influencers and decision makers that there are no outstanding product, service or administration issues.(AR's)
  2. That key influencers and decision makers in the ACCOUNT acknowledge that YOU have been able to rectify any previous issues to their satisfaction.
Examples
  • A previous product release had significant reliability issues and YOU were able to quickly provide alternative, reliable products.
  • That while YOU failed in certain service issues, YOU were able to demonstrate the resource available to offer both the expertise and the capacity to resolve these.
Considerations
  • Always look for the positive in any situation. YOUR ability to resolve issues efficiently is sometimes more important than having no issues at all.
  • Remember to make sure the key influencers and decision makers are aware of the positive service benefits of working with YOU, including the fact that YOU have been able to address issues efficiently.
