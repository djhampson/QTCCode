2. Significant Wins or Losses

Question
Have we learned anything regarding strategy and tactics with the customer from any significant previous wins or losses? What are the implications for YOUR future success in this customer’s specific market?
Objective
To review previous major bids and to understand the root causes for their success or failure in order to tailor future offerings.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. Detail previous wins and losses and document the reasons for success and failure.
  2. Determine what the implications are for future opportunities.
  3. Clarify the points YOU have learned with key influencers and decision makers in the ACCOUNT.
Examples
  • All successful opportunities had economic buyer sponsorship.
  • The majority of unsuccessful opportunities failed to agree a schedule of events.
  • YOU have a poor service record within the ACCOUNT.
Considerations
  • YOU are looking to see what YOU can learn from previous bids.
  • Look beyond the usual product / feature / price argument. Question the sales strategy and tactics.
