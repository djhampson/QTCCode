14. People

Question
Do e have the right people deployed in our virtual team with the skill sets required to achieve our ambition?
Objective
To ensure we have the competency and skills needed to execute the Plan2Prosper.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. You have documented the roles and responsibilities of those in the virtual team.
  2. You have identified those people that you need in the virtual team.
  3. You have identified the skills gap that exists between those you have identified and the needs of the plan.
  4. You have identified any training or recruitment needs.
Examples
  • In order to attain the Sustainable Mutual Business Value (SMBV) we need to offer consultative services to help the customer identify future business needs in relation to our products and services.
Considerations
  • Consider the product mix of product, price, support and services.
