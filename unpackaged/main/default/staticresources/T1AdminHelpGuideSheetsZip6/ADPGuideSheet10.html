6. Business Relationship

Question
Do we have a business relationship goal? (Supplier, preferred supplier, trusted advisor or business partner). Which is our relationship strategy? With which of the key decision makers are YOU appropriately positioned? Who are our immediate targets in the political map?
Objective
To determine an appropriate relationship objective and to ensure we have a network of key contacts in the customer through which you can demonstrate the value we offer and who can drive the decision making processes of the major opportunities that we will be pursuing.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. That we have determined our current supplier status.
  2. That we have determined our objective for supplier status.
  3. That we have mapped the formal organization charts.
  4. That we have mapped the matrix of relationships between us and our contacts within the customer.
  5. That we have mapped the informal relationships that exist.
  6. That we have developed a relationship development plan that leverages the informal relationships in the customer.
Examples
That in order to attain Business Partner status, we need to develop a relationship between our CEO and that of the customer so they become a point of referral for future bids and initiatives. Our tactic for achieving this is through the informal connection of our VP Ops with a non-executive of the customer’s Board of Directors.
Considerations
  • The relationship development plan can exist on many levels, building contacts at both senior and junior level as you will need a variety of points of reference.
  • You will need to build relationships not only for helping with sales opportunities, but also to help manage through times of difficulty and when the ACCOUNT is under threat from competition.
  • YOUR future goals and ambitions will drive the types of relationship you need to develop. It might not be appropriate to want to achieve Business Partner status in every circumstance.
  • YOU need to earn the right to YOUR business relationship status. YOU might need to work YOUR way through the individual stages rather than go directly for the status YOU are targeting in the long term.
  • Who your main competition is will also drive the type of relationship YOU need to build.
