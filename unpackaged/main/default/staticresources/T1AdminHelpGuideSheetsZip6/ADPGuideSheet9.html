5. Sustainable Mutual Business Value

Question
Do we have agreement with our customer with respect to a sustainable, mutual business value - the ability to create a win / win for our two organizations within a three year time frame?
Objective
To determine a long term goal (typically three years) that has clear, sometimes strategic, benefits to the customer in helping them reach their own objectives and TechOne sustaining the type of business that will help you achieve our goals.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. That we have determined and can articulate the Long Term Sustainable Mutual Business Value.
  2. That we have agreement to that goal with both key influencers and economic decision makers within the customer.
  3. That wehave agreed a milestone plan with the key economic sponsors.
Examples
  • That through the provision of our product and services, we can help our customer improve sales productivity and transition the customer from being product driven to customer led.
Considerations
  • The joint ambition should have a two year horizon as this is both long enough to determine genuine change and short enough to have operational benefits that will accrue in time for the current management team.
  • Mutuality is key – if both parties benefit they will work enthusiastically to the same end.
