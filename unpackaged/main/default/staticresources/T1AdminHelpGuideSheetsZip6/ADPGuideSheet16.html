16. Permission

Question
Do you have the required executive sanction to pursue our ambition?
Objective
To ensure we have the appropriate authority to pursue our sales opportunities and the milestone plan in order that you have the necessary backing to invest funds and resources in pursuit of our ambition.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. That you can detail the key sales opportunities and milestone objectives.
  2. That you have determined the resources required and the cost of pursuing these objectives.
  3. That you have documented evidence that YOU have the permission to commit the resources to achieve the objectives.
Examples
  • You have documentary evidence that your Executive approve the allocation of resource for your account development plan.
Considerations
  • Gaining permission ensures you can count on the necessary support when and where it is needed.
  • Pursuing your objectives without permission may jeopardise your business relationship and internal credibility.
