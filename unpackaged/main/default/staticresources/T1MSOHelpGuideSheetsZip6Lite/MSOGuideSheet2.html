2. Business Action / Process

Question
Has the prospect/customer initiated a formal response to address the opportunity or threat that the pain/gain represents?
Objective
To establish that the prospect or customer is committing business resources to address the business pressure(s).
  • Discover if a project has been sponsored to evaluate solutions to the business pressures.
  • Determine which named individuals have been tasked with evaluating solutions to the business pressures.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. Clear evidence that the prospect or customer is (or is planning to be) engaged in initiatives designed to address the drivers uncovered in #1, above.
  2. Strong indications that our products/services can potentially support or augment these initiatives.
Examples
External
  • Acquiring business to establish market share and economies.
  • Re-positioning business to provide margin enhancing solutions.
Internal
  • Evaluating MIS systems in support of business processes.
  • Developing skills to support a change agenda.
    •
Considerations
  • None
Links
  • #1 – If the responses are not linked to the drivers, ask yourself why are they talking to you about this project/initiative.
