12. Win Plan

Question
Do we have a win plan to close the deal?
Objective
To determine a strategy and the timing of implementing that strategy that will give the best possible chance for the sales bid to succeed:-
  • Direct. (Head on against the specified buying criteria of the prospect / customer).
  • Indirect. (Changing the goal posts by seeking to influence the criteria).
  • Fragment. (Co-exist with competitor or establish a beachhead and expand).
  • Partner. (Join forces with a competitor).
  • Defer. (Delay the decision in order to invest time in researching / PR and building relationships).
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. Evidence of approach to win the opportunity.
  2. Evidence of our strengths and weaknesses in the oppportunity
  3. Evidence of any out "out of ordinary" actions
Examples
  • Direct – your relative performance against the buying criteria is better than the competition.
  • Indirect – you are generally weak against the buying criteria but significantly stronger in a particular area.
  • Fragment – you cannot satisfy the total requirements of the prospect / customer but can satisfy a component better than the competition and restrict our bid to that requirement.
  • Partner – your weaknesses are complemented by a competitor and vice versa.
  • Defer - your bid is inferior but will be stronger with some further investment of time and resource.
Considerations
  • Have we delivered our company’s standard contract or is this non-standard?
  • Will your offer meet compliance requirements?
