6. Positioning

Question
Is our virtual team positioned with the key influencers and decision makers and are you using these relationships to win the opportunity?
Objective
To leverage the network of our virtual team with PROSPECT representatives in order to influence key decision makers and gain support for our solution.
  • To use our Personal Insight to ensure our sponsor promotes you as a ‘trusted advisor’ to key influencers in the decision.
  • To use our Political Insight to create awareness in key influencers of our strengths and our competitors weaknesses.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. Our internal sponsors are advocating our value to key decision makers.
  2. Informal influencers including our Executive team are positioned with the relevant people in their network with regard to the benefits of our solution.
Examples
  • Key influencers providing details of key hot spots areas/buying criteria.
  • Keyinfluencer/decision maker providing information on competition.
Considerations
  • Do we have the appropriate internal (to us) Executive sponsors engaged?
  • It is the role of the lead Account Manager to both position and exploit all mapped relationships.
  • ALL of the virtual team is beholden to the Account Manager to orchestrate the positioning and associated activities to achieve that alignment.
