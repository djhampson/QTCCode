5. Competitive Differentiation

Question
Have you determined a differentiated value proposition that addresses the buying criteria and positions us to win the opportunity versus the competition?
Objective
To establish as strong (relative to customer needs and financially) and as differentiated a value proposition as possible that addresses the buying criteria and other (stated and non-stated) critical needs of the prospect / customer).
  • Articulate our Value Proposition in the prospect / customers terms.
  • Order our benefits as points of parity (same as the competition) and points of difference.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. We have built a value proposition that delivers the benefits being sought, addresses the key drivers and highlights our main features.
  2. Our offer is differentiated and aimed at the critical needs, including those that have resonating focus (areas of benefit that can be proven through significant previous experience).
Examples
  • Frame the value proposition as:
    "You will be able to deploy world class sales practice and make a step change in ROI of your Saleforce.com investment in order to gain market share through improved sales conversion rates and increase gross profit between using Plan2Close which is native with Salesforce.com and increases sales teams adoption because of its framework of excellence that enables the iterative process of selling.’’
Considerations
  • Do we know where we have delivered value of this type before with this client?
  • Have we been successful with similar bids with different clients?
  • What have we learned from similar bids?
