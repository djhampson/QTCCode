10. Alignment

Question
Are you able to demonstrate our organisation’s value that we can deliver through the sales process and the delivery of the solution?
Objective
To demonstrate alignment by highlighting that there is a good fit between our organization and that of the prospect / customer and that we are delivering value during the sales cycle in terms of the people we deploy in the areas of:
  • Expertise (do we add value in terms of the knowledge you bring?).
  • Culture (what do they reward, what do they punish?).
  • Values (how they behave and what they expect - published or observed).
  • Systems (how we both do business/SLAs/operational support).
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. Uncover the expectations of the client in respect of value received during the sales cycle in terms of the people we deploy, the knowledge and expertise we bring and our attitude.
  2. Evidence the prospect / customer values the capabilities you bring and the characteristics you display.
Examples
  • One of this prospect’s values voiced as speed of response. Ensure that you are tuned to this expectation and deliver accordingly during the sales cycle.
  • This prospect is hi-tech in terms of systems and our solution is a good fit given its use of technology.
  • During the sales cycle the client has high expectations for clear standards and values associated with the way you conduct your business, your company’s values and culture.
