8. Strategic Value

Question
Is there strategic value to you if you win the opportunity?
Objective
Determine the strategic business value and/or future revenue to be gained from winning this opportunity.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. There is a clear path to significant (for you) revenue within a timescale considered to be acceptable by you and your management.
    and/or
  2. This is potentially a highly significant “flagship” customer whose market visibility is likely to attract further revenue from similar businesses supported by references.
    and/or
  3. This the first deal of its kind from which you will learn a significant amount which will enable you and your company to address similar customers/markets.
Examples
  • Strategic Value – taking you into a new market.
  • Future Revenue – other opportunities in the pipeline worth $xxx over next 12 months – maybe supported by successful visibility of this deal.
Considerations
  • If short term revenue is low, then there should be future revenue or strategic value in pursuing this opportunity.
  • Would this make a good reference story? Have we gained agreement for this prospect / customer to provide approval for publicity? Have they made similar PR arrangements before?
