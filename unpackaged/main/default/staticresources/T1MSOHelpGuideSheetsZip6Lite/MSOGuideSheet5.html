5. Client Capability / Capacity

Question
Is the prospect / customer capable of both project managing the decision and the implementation of the solution?
Objective
To ensure the prospect / customer has the skills and levels of resource to successfully project manage the decision and the implementation of any solution. If not, then...
  • To determine the additional resource we will need to supply and subsequent cost to us.
  • To ensure you have management authority to commit that resource.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. Confirmation that the client has the resources required to manage both the decision and the implementation. If not....
  2. Clear evaluation of any support resource that you will have to give during the sales cycle, the likely time and cost, and....
  3. Agreement with your management that the above costs are within normal parameters and feasible.
Examples
  • Complex solution – prospect / customer has limited internal resource – require project management support – can we sell this in?
Considerations
  • If we are engaging with partners are contracts in place?
  • If there is a risk, do we need to provide extra resource and if so, at what cost?
