13. Visibility/Credibility

Question
Are we known by the most influential people in the prospect / customer and are we credible enough to positively affect the decision?
Objective
  • To secure the prospect / customers full awareness (aiming to reach trusted advisor status) of our capabilities, potential and value already delivered at all relevant levels of influence.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. We are well perceived throughout the ‘decision tree’ relating to this opportunity at executive, senior management and operational levels.
  2. If there are reputation or awareness/education issues to address, we have implemented a plan to address them.
  3. The prospect / customer is aware of the ‘value add’ of our product and service range,
    including past value delivered by YOU both to the client and to others in their industry
    sector.
  4. Our Executive team are engaged with the appropriate people
Examples
  • Key decision makers worked with us before – no issues and commended us on our response to their issues.
  • Key influencers previously had issues with us – but we have a plan to get a contact who has worked well with us to help us address these issues.
Considerations
  • Do we have a track record for previous deals we have bid with this prospect / customer?
