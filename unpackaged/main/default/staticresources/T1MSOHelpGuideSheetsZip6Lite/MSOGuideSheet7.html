7. Ability to Respond

Question
Do we have the time, competence and resource in order to run the sales plan?
Objective
Establish that we can put together a suitable bid or determine if anything is likely to delay / jeopardise our response and the nature of the threat to the deal if this is not resolved.
  • Practically, in terms of responding to requests, RFP’s, pricing…
  • In terms of having time / resources to build the necessary relationships
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. We have the time and resources to meet any response deadlines set by the prospect.
  2. Do the Product General Managers, Solution Managers and other teams have the
    required resources available to respond appropriately? Has this been discussed with them?
  3. We are not compromising a quality response through lack of time to deliver it.
  4. We have access to the skills we need to respond and deliver appropriately.
Examples
  • We have confirmation from all those needed to answer that they can respond on time.
  • We wish to clarify a specific requirement with a senior decision maker and we have the time to approach him/her.
  • We have access to a specialist resource to address a specific requirement.
Considerations
  • If YOU do not have the required time, ascertain the likelihood that the prospect / customer will give us more time.
  • Have you engaged bid/support resources to meet the deadlines imposed by the prospect / customer?
  • Do we have agreement with any partners on the feasibility of their meeting response timescales?
