14. Personal Insight

Question
Are you connected to a key supporter to the extent that you are aware of their and other’s personal and business agendas?
Objective
To gain an ‘inside sponsor / coach’ by uncovering intelligence, via triangulation of prospect / customer resources, about key company and relevant individual personal goals.
  • To determine who has what role in the decision making process.
  • To determine if we can help them achieve their key goals through the inherent value offered by our products and services, our personal added value or those of your personal network.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. We are very closely connected with at least one influential customer representative and they are aware that we can help them achieve their own key aspirations and ultimately those of their organization.
Examples
  • Key influencers need to be seen as heroes – e.g. to be seen to reduce costs by $xxx and reduce suppliers to 2. They do not necessarily know all decision makers but rely on influencers with whom they are well connected.
  • Know key decision maker – brought in to transform customer service – issues are reliability – does not know whether to have 2/3 providers – past experience has shown that you need back up. Looking to expand into Europe but does not know how to best achieve this...looking for help.
Considerations
  • Can you move your relationship to the point where you contact is seeking advice or knowledge or help in attaining their personal goals.
