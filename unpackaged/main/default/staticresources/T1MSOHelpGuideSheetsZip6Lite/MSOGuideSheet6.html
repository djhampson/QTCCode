6. Deal value

Question
Is the deal value if we win this opportunity worth the resource you will need to commit to win it?
Objective
To determine if the opportunity has sufficient value in the short term to justify investment in the sales cycle or....
  • In the event that the above is untrue, consider the strategic value or future revenue in order to justify progressing with this sale and report it in #8.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. Revenue from this deal will be equivalent to or above the minimum considered to be acceptable.
  2. The timeframe is within our horizon to warrant committing resources.
Examples
  • Short term revenue does not support investment but a win would be a significant
    entry in to a new industry segment / customer or product range.
Considerations
  • Resource availability
  • Other projects in progress
  • Strategy for product / market
  • Risks
Links
  • #8 - If Deal value is low then does the long term strategic value justify progressing the sale?
