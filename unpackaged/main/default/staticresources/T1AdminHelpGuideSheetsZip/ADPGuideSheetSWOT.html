ValuMatrix

Purpose
To determine the Sustainable Mutual Business Value (SMBV) that will create a win/win for YOU and YOUR ACCOUNT and around which YOU can focus YOUR account development plan, the Plan2Prosper.
Description
Based on the classic strategic planning tool with a twist, the SWOT Analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities and Threats), the ValuMatrix considers the external Opportunities and Threats of the ACCOUNT and YOUR internal Strengths in order for YOU to identify the SMBV.
Steps in the Process
  1. In the Opportunities tab, click on the drop down list of evidence from certain questions in the 4x4, craft Opportunity statements for the ACCOUNT. Also craft opportunity statements from information that you know in general. Quantify the Opportunity by evaluating its scale (small or large) and the degree that the ACCOUNT is currently aligned (low or high) to it. (That is; is the opportunity business as usual (fully aligned) or will it require the ACCOUNT to invest in new products and services).
  2. Repeat the process for Threats to the ACCOUNT.
  3. Repeat the process for YOUR Strengths.
  4. Click on the Sustainable Mutual Business Value tab, and review the Opportunities and Threats of the ACCOUNT alongside YOUR strengths.
  5. Craft the SMBV statement in the template - The ACCOUNT will be able to… (maximise opportunities) and… (counter threats… (by working together with YOU who… (highlight strengths)…
Considerations
  • ACCOUNT opportunities and threats relate to market share and margin and may be as a result of the competitive landscape (CompetingForces) and political, economic, sociological and technological (PEST) reasons.
  • SMBV should be a three year horizon (current and next two) – a tangible goal that YOU and the ACCOUNT can work toward.
