4. Relationship Status

Question
Do the key influencers and economic decision makers in the customer account know about the past value we have delivered, the major wins we have gained and the positive service experience they have had?
Objective
To ensure that the key influencers and decision makers understand the value YOU can bring and are aware of the resources that are available to YOU.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. li>Prove that there is Executive engagement in the customer account
  2. Prove that YOU have delivered significant business value to the ACCOUNT.
  3. Detail the significant wins and losses.
  4. Highlight the resolution of major service issues.
  5. Demonstrate that the key influencers and economic buyers are aware of these facts.
Examples
  • You have helped the ACCOUNT overcome significant operational issues as a result of a defect in your product in a timely and efficient manner.
  • The service issue had been escalated to senior management.
  • Resolution was acknowledged by the ACCOUNT’s senior management.
Considerations
  • The significant value derived needs to be quantifiable.
  • Services issues are not necessarily bad news. YOUR ability to respond in a timely and efficient manner can differentiate YOU from the competition.
Links
  • #1 – Significant value derived so that YOU are armed with the positives of your previous engagement.
  • #2 – Significant wins and losses so you understand the history of your bids.
  • #3 – Service history so that you know the reputation YOU will have.
  • OrgChartPlus - so YOU can target key people in YOUR ACCOUNT.
