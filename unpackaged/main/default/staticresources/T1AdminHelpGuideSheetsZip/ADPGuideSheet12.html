12. Revenue Objectives

Question
What are the revenue targets for this customer for the next two years?
Objective
To determine and gain agreement to the fiscal objectives for the current and subsequent year
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. Revenue target for this year licences and services.
  2. Revenue target for next year licences and services.
Examples
  • Revenue by product.
  • Revenue by services.
Considerations
  • Do not set targets for metrics that you cannot measure.
