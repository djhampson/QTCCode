1. Significant Value Achieved

Question
Can we identify the business value the customer has already derived from TechOne?
Description
To determine whether we are able to reference significant business value to establish our credibility in the customer account.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. Clear evidence that we have delivered significant business value in the past.
  2. Clear evidence that the value wehave delivered is acknowledged by the customer.
Examples
  • We have been able to work with the customer in specific parts of their business and they acknowledge quantified business benefits as a result.
  • The benefits are also acknowledged as helping them respond to key (sometimes strategic) business drivers.
  • Willingnes to be utilised as a reference site.
  • Participation in a Case Study
Considerations
  • YOU may consider significant, quantifiable, business benefits that YOU have delivered to other organizations.
  • Of particular value is if YOU can refer to significant value delivered in areas that the ACCOUNT has not yet articulated, pointing out a business opportunity that they are not yet aware of.
  • you may want to consider non-quantifiable benefits such as important information you have been able to impart.
