6. Business Drivers

Question
Have we identified the external (market/legislative) and internal (operational/financial) business drivers?
Objective
To determine what is affecting the customer from both an internal and external point of view.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. To be able to explain the major business drivers for the customer and what affect these are having.
  2. To have validated this ‘scenario’ with key influencers and decision makers in the ACCOUNT.
Examples
  • External: Market demand has declined due to the economic slowdown, with the consequent effect of tightening margins.
  • External: Legislation has changes resulting in additional reporting requirements.
  • Internal: The customer has merged with its largest competitor and there is a programme of consolidation.
  • Internal: The share price is underperforming the market and the customer and market analysts are looking for an improvement in market share.
Considerations
  • What is the economic environment?
  • What is the legislative environment?
  • What are the dynamics of the market?
