13. Product

Question
Are our products and solutions ready for delivery to support our Ambition for this customer.
Objective
Having considered the critical needs and the Sustainable Mutual Business Value (SMBV) - to ensure we have the necessary products, solutions and services in order to attain the SMBV.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. That we have an agreed SMBV.
  2. That we have identified our service and product requirements to attain the SMBV.
Examples
  • Provision of a 24 hour support line enables customer to increase their operational hours and revenue.
  • That our CIAnywhere suite is ready to deploy ECM
Considerations
  • Consider the product mix – product features / quality, price, support and service.
