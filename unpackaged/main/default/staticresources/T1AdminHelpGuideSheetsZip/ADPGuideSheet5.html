5. Business Goals

Question
What are the customers key goals and ambition? With whom and with what evidence do you support these?
Objective
Having established the business drivers, the objective is to determine the customer's strategic intent in order to put our future plans in to context. To be able to articulate how the strategic intent drives initiatives, or business responses by the customer.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. Articulate the strategic intent of the ACCOUNT both in terms of overall market positioning and in financial terms.
  2. To have validated the strategic intent with key influencers and decision makers.
Examples
  • The ACCOUNT intends to become a market leader and is broadening the product and service offering to include solutions and services.
Considerations
  • Consider the time frame of the stated intent. The more long term the less likely the degree of urgency. In which case watch out for more short term business responses (see question 8).
