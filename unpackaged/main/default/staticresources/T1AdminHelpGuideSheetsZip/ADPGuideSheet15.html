15. Support

Question
Do we have the necessary support to execute the Plan2Prosper?
Objective
To ensure wehave both sales management and cross functional support that ensures you have the means to execute the Plan2Prosper.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. That we have documented and quantified the cost of the sales and cross functional support we require to achieve our ambition in the Plan2Prosper.
  2. That we have agreement from all relevant management for the products and resources needed.
  3. That the agreement is documented.
Examples
  • That we require 24 x 7 support globally.
  • That we have agreement from the OO Products and Solutions.
  • That this is documented in an e-mail.
Considerations
  • Think about the product mix when determining the support YOU will require – Product features / quality, price, service and support.
