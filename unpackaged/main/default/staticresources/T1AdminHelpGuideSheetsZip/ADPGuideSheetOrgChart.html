OrgChartPlus

Purpose
OrgChartPlus is the means by which to determine who and how to influence in the Plan2Prosper.
Description
OrgChartPlus is the means to determine who in the ACCOUNT has what role in the decision making process, what their opinions of us are, their relationship with OUR virtual team and the dynamics that exist between them.
  • OrgChartPlus allows YOU to document the organizations of the various business units covered in YOUR Plan2Prosper.
  • The Matrix enables YOU to plot contacts in specific organizations of YOUR ACCOUNT, YOUR virtual team and understand the influence and opinion of YOUR contacts and their relation with YOUR virtual team.
  • The OrgChart helps YOU map the formal decision making ‘organization’ determining the key players and mind mapping the various relationships.
  • The Political Web looks at YOU and YOUR team’s sphere of influence in the ACCOUNT and helps YOU work through the politics to advance YOUR plan.
Steps in the Process
  • In The Matrix, plot the key people in the ACCOUNT and YOUR virtual team.
  • Determine and record the influence the contact has on you developing YOUR plan, their opinion of YOU and the type of relationship they have with members of YOUR virtual team.
  • In The OrgChart, organize the contacts in a formal hierarchy of who reports to whom and who YOU need to influence as part of the Plan2Prosper.
  • Mind map the relationships that exist between the contacts in order to capture the informal politics that will have a major influence. Determine who YOU need to influence in order to promote YOUR Long Term Sustainable Mutual Business Value.
  • In the Political Web establish who in YOUR virtual team has the closest relationship and what the individual relationships are. Consider how YOU can use both the formal hierarchy and informal politics.
  • Using OrgChartPlus record the relevant answers in questions 4 and 10.
Considerations
  • Remember the intent of questions 4 and 10. That is to determine that YOU have exposure at the right levels in the ACCOUNT and that YOU know both the formal and informal environment that YOU are selling in and to and finally, how YOU want to position YOURSELF.
  • Also consider outside influences – namely external consultants or business partners.
Links
