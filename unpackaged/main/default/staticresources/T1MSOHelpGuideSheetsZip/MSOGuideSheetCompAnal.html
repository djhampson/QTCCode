SalesMaster

Purpose
To direct you tothe elements of Plan2Close that are relevant at each of TechOne's stages of the Sale
and to alert you to those elements that need addressing if you are to move forward in the Sale.
Description
SalesMaster is a real time assessment of the state of the sales opportunity plan in relation to your
claimed stage of the sale. Having determined the critiera that ought to be satisfied at each stage,
SalesMaster both details what should have been achieved and what needs to be achieved next.
Process
Having decided to move to the next stage of the sale;
  • Click on the previous stage to establish if there are any criteria that should have been achieved but have not been
    as denoted by a blue warning triangle.
  • If there are, then you have the option of clicking on the relevant question number link and
    navigating to the question to either amend the status and evidence or set new objectives
    and task in order to achieve the criteria.
  • For the current stage, with a click on the stage ‘chevron’ to establish which questions need
    to be addressed, navigating as appropriate, or reference the 4x4 matrix and address those
    questions that have been emboldened.
  • While questions that do not have to be addressed at the current stage are greyed out,
    you may still address these if the opportunity to do so arises. (Remember; Plan2Close is a
    framework rather than an algorythm in order that you can action any component should the
    opportunity arise.
Considerations
  • Remember that Plan2Close is as sales methodology and not a sales process. Stages of the
    sale are what TechOne regard as your sales process. SalesMaster simply aligns its discipline
    with TechOne’s stages of the sale.
