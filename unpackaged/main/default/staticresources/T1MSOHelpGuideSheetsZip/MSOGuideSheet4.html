4. Compelling Event

Question
Are there clear consequences for the client to implement a solution by a specific event so that the decision has to be made by a specific time?
Objective
To uncover CLEAR and EXPLICIT indications that they must do this project by a set time:
  • Determine what will happen if they do not do this project within a specific timeframe and what pain or detriment they will suffer… or what potential gain they may fail to realise.
  • Determine what we can do to help the prospect or customer understand the importance of this project.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  • Explicit demonstration by the prospect or customer that there is a compelling, time specific, event – driven entirely by their own impetus – and that the consequences of non-delivery/achievement or success will have significant impact.
Examples
  • Have to reduce operating costs by 10% by end of year or job on the line (Personal pain – is there a business pain too?)
  • New offices opening on Aug 30th – if systems not in place then offices will be inoperable
Considerations
  • If there is no compelling event then can you create one?
Links
