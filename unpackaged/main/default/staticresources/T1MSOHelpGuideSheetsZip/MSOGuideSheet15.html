15. Political Insight

Question
Do you understand the non-formal as well as formal decision making process and the key influences?
Objective
To determine how the decisions are influenced politically.
  • Determine whether decision making process is really as formal as we have been told.
  • Establish true power structure – not just in project decisions, but generally.
  • Uncover who influences whom.
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. Political Map showing you/prospect / customer relationship and your prospect / customers informal relationships.
  2. We are very closely connected with at least two influential prospect / customer representatives.
Examples
  • VP Technology used to work with CEO in previous company. You are aligned with the VP Technology who, while not directly involved in the decision making process is aware of the benefits you bring.
  • CEO is close to an external consultant who has worked with our company in assignments with other companies and is aware of past value we have delivered.
Considerations
  • Consider 3rd party relationships and influences/ers – who are they connected with?
