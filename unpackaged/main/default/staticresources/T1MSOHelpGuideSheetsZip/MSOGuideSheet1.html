1. Point of Pain / Gain

Question
Why does the prospect or customer need to take action? What is the issue/opportunity driving them to look around?
Objective
To establish that there are tangible reasons (business drivers) for the PROSPECT to seek alternate options.
  • Discover external forces on their business (e.g. Market/Competition).
  • Discover internal forces on their business (e.g. Operational/Financial).
Criteria to achieve GREEN
  1. Clear evidence that the point of pain/gain could have a fundamental affect on the success of the organization.
  2. Clear evidence the business drivers are acknowledged by the prospect/customer.
  3. Clear evidence that the TechOne products / services can impact these.
Examples
External
  • Market in decline due to economy is causing sales to decline.
  • New competition in the market with new technology / service is taking market share.
Internal
  • Organization not keeping pace with growth – systems are struggling.
  • Organization merger is leading to re-structuring and changing business processes.
Considerations
  • There may be one or more business pressures – in addition to contact questioning use
    information from their accounts,Internet and financial press to see if you can uncover
    their business pressures.
